Services

Check-Up

We have a proprietary St. Ives Health Screening Program (SIHSP).

Immunization

St. Ives offers the EPI every 1st and 3rd Thursday of every month (9am to 12pm) to children.

ICSI

Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection is the most successful treatment for male infertility
PGT

Preimplantation Genetic Testing helps prevent known genetic abnormalities from being transmitted from parent to child
Insemination

Intrauterine insemination (IUI) is the placing of sperm into a woman's uterus when she is ovulating.
Ultrasound Scan

Ultrasound scans are used to evaluate fetal development, and they can detect problems in the body.

Why St Ives Specialist Hospital

We offer dedicated specialist services in all matters of women, children and family health, managed and delivered by a group of consultants in obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics, medicine and surgery.

Each operational arm is situated at seperate locations, well equipped with modern facilities, and well staffed with qualified specialists and professionals who are abrest of modern trends, global best practices and developments in their various specialties

St. Ives also provides referrals to affiliate medical experts abroad on occasions when required.
